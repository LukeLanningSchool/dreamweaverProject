About

Donny's Diner is a local 50s themed diner with great music and even greater times to be had here! We at Donny's Diner are a family and consider you a major part of it. We hope you enjoy our food and take a trip back to the 50s with us!

From food and music to fun events, we have everything to entertain all ages. We proudly are made in the USA and strive to give the best experiences possible!

While we may be a small new restaurant, we hope you will come and enjoy our local slice of the 50s with us!
